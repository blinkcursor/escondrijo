The hammock room

The Hammock room is an impressive double-height room with stairs via a mezzanine level out to a sunny private terrace.

The room features a large open-plan living and sleeping area, with an en-suite bathroom.

It offers very generous space for a couple, and the sofa is a standard double-sized sofa-bed which comfortably accommodates 1 adult or 2 children making it ideal for families, too.

There is a stereo with iPod connector & CD library, and plenty of space and facilities for preparing snacks and drinks. We provide beach towels and parasols so you can travel light.

WiFi is fast and free.
