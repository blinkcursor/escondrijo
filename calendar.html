Vejer de la Frontera

A largely independent suite with generous private terrace on the top floor of a beautiful and historic patio house which until recently was run full-time as a guesthouse, rated #1 on Tripadvisor.

The L-shaped suite has a large comfortable double-bed plus a sofa-bed suitable for a child. The en-suite bathroom has a funky sunken shower and the mini-kitchenette has all you need for making drinks and cold snacks. There is a BBQ on the terrace, too.

Costa de la Luz

The terrace has wonderful uninterrupted views across the old-town to the surrounding countryside, with the mountains of the Alcornocales natural park beyond. On clear days you can see the Rif mountains of Morocco across the Straits of Gibraltar.

The room has a flat-screen TV with UK freesat and Spanish TV, and there is a stereo which can double as your iPod speakers. Hassle-free WiFi is included.

The bedding and towels are our hotel-grade high quality stock, and we also provide complementary beach towels and parasols.

The house is located in a quiet pedestrian lane at the heart of the old town, and Vejer has many fabulous bars and restaurants just a short stroll away.
